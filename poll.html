Selection procedures

1. All registered members are the voters for SC. Each Voter will be asked to give 3 preferences for SC. A separate voting form is being prepared for each batch and will be circulated In'sha Allah.

2. A voter SHOULD NOT vote for himself. Also, a person should give 3 different preferences (Giving same person's name for all 3 preferences is not allowed)

3. Persons securing maximum votes from each batch will be counted and top 2 persons from each batch will be selected for SHURA COUNCIL.

4. The SC will have 6 members. One out of six members will be selected as Amir. SC members will then vote and select the Amir amongst themselves.

5. Now the Shura Council will have one vacant post as a SC member became Amir. This vacancy will be filled by the THIRD HIGHEST VOTE SECURING PERSON from the Amir's batch.

6. Amir will be a neutral person and the Shura Council will be represented equally by all batches.
